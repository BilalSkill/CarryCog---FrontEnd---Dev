About Us

WHAT DRIVES US

CarryCog - Vision

CarryCog is an online community based logistic system.
Which can help in exchanging packages to and from other countries without hustling for high logistic charges and the best part - No third party is involved
CarryCog is a platform where travelers can make money while travelling, by delivering packages to the desired destination chosen by requester.
CarryCog can help you get in touch with individuals.

Either by posting your route details.
OR
You can search and send request on existing posts from all over the world.
