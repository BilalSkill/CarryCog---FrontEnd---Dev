CarryCog Frequently Asked Questions.

Note: Following are some of the Frequently Asked Questions on CarryCog you can always reach out to us by sending us your message on Contact Us

What is CarryCog?

CarryCog is an online community based logistic system.

How CarryCog is helpful for me?

CarryCog can help you in exchanging packages to and from any countries without hustling for high logistic charges and without any third party involvement.

In which country CarryCog is operational?

Since CarryCog is a community based system and operating on cloud, hence it is accesable globaly.

How to join CarryCog?

You can sign up for for free with your email ID Sign up now

How do I decide Cost/KG while posting travel details?

Decision is yours CarryCog does not force/limit you. Be realistic while charging for services.

How to get in touch?

CarryCog can help you get in touch with individuals.
Either by posting your route details.
OR
You can search and send request on existing posts from all over the world.

How can I know my request has been accepted?

Whenever traveller/requester reply back to your request/offer that means your request for service has been accepted.

Will CarryCog take any guarantee of my parcel?

CarryCog is a community based logistic system it helps you get in touch with traveller / requesters but does not guarantee for any deal done between individuals.

How will I know what items are restricted to Carry on Airport?

You can find restricted item from your airline helpline or from world IATA website.
You can find some of item listed in our blog post Restricted Items

How do we ensure someone's authenticity?

Both parties should exchange a guarantor in between and should ask for local / destination reference ID card.

What payment channel will be used while charging for parcel delivery?

Individuals can charge in local currency by COD or any money transfer convenient for both parties.
